Don't Let it Slip

Sara Chu

Don’t Let it Slip by Sara Chu is an immersive interactive game experience about navigating different stages of life. The main objective of this game is not to win, but to focus on life’s smaller details. From baby to adult stages, the user gains perspective on various generational memories and hardships. Chu prompts users to realize how fleeting life can be. Memories of when she would play games on her iPod touch for hours and get lost in those virtual realities inspired Chu to create this project. Don't Let it Slip has allowed Chu to take her relation to gaming and virtual worlds full circle by creating her own gaming experience.
